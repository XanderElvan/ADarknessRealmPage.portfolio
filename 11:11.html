11:11 butterflies all the way

It’s a half-moon birthday
joie de vivre d'autrefois

yesteryear mismatched
with an innate desire to not die alone
vast scale ever-tipping
the topography of consciousness

Dragon fire-breath is a debate -
survival of the fittest
trapped with a vengeance

like parallel processing
or the psychology of traffic

"That's where I used to come from,"
said the fallen leaf
out of all the places
to cross paths

“the aim is to slow down,
isn't that the idea?"
respond a collective forest
with natural intuition.

A guide to instinct;
some sort of pamphlet
on the external world
tuned to string theory frequencies -

It’s not what we truly want, but it’s what
we’re meant to have - snowdrift dreams
freer in open spaces -
the other little worlds that I can see through

the bigger pictures
in the smallest detail

a compelling element
and an epiphany for the day
(p.s. it’s about time)
