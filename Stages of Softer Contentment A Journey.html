Stages of Softer Contentment:
A Journey

Water Boatman
learn the process of mistakes,
finding their testa-feet.

Breeze-woven birdsong lingers
A question: are you safety
or preying disguised?

As lullabies tighten overhead, Tuesday's
orange progression towards dusk
presents a late hope of reaching foundation

Exoskeletons breathe out, adrenaline
singing vein chords toward cloud
too distant to catch punctuation

Life’s current is voiced in the melee
of wings, beaks and wings, beating
their verse: necessity, live

or die. Silence
sinks across a pondside,
Witness to the next turn

Water Boatman breathe in survivor’s currency
already stitching experience into memory,
hearts, wings and minds settling to mirror the tide

Sometimes the reward for your adventure
isn't the treasure you wanted, but a greater one
peace of mind.
