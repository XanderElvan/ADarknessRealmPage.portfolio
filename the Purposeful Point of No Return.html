Stages of Softer Contentment: A Journey without soundscape:

- the Purposeful Point of No Return,


as Hivemind neurons collapse their way across Asteroid Fields,
cerebral retrograde reaches atmosphere-pace.

thoughtful refuelling in Negative sub-space will later ignite
providing thrust, opposing the motion of Solitude.

Without instruction, Withdrawn
boundless and detached- Warped

inexorably closer towards -Centres of the Universe-

places where planets once ruled.

now an Expanse of recalcitrant Ion Trails
unashamedly gazing upon
the Ideals of Creation

The Mind have stretched back and covered the Span of Time
to pinpoint a blackhole,
surveying the full breadth and multitude of known perimeters.

and yet, You somehow find something new



for All i Care -
