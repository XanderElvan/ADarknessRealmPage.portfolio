Twenty questions with your character:


Below you are twenty questions you can use to get to know one of your characters better. Above you, you will find three different soundscapes, each capturing the atmosphere from one of The Darkness Realm’s worlds.

We invite you to experiment with any combination of the questions and sounds offered here, to see if this can ignite new ideas, or reveal more depth to one of your pre-existing characters.

There is no, ‘right way’ to go about this, if one of the soundscapes speaks to you, you might find yourself having a conversation with a new character that lives there. You might disregard the soundscapes altogether, instead taking this opportunity to sit down with one of your characters and get to know them a little better.

What’s your name?


How old are you?


What’s you gender?


Where were you born and raised?


Where do you live now?


What can you see out of your window?


What are you really good at?


What’s your biggest weakness?


What are you afraid of?


What’s your proudest accomplishment?


What’s your worst habit?


What’s your guilty pleasure?


What’s your most prized possession?


What makes you happy?


What makes you angry?


What can you do that would surprise your friends?


What do you really want?


Why are you here, now?
(either answer generally: What’s led you to this point in your life?
Or, more specifically: Why are you doing this interview?)


What have you never told anyone before?


What’s your dream job?


And last, but not least, what have you got in your pocket?
