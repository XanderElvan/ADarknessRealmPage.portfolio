Components of a Letter

Mereology

Keyhole valleys
in corrosive land-
locked corridors
and foundation wallpaper
border-lines a rite of passage.

One fraction of the place beyond-
circumtance is
more than a pilgrimage
through a single doorway.

Ever-thinning strip of landing lights
beckon an inquisitive entrance
which only oak and brass may separate.

An unfamiliar grain of dependency,
inner-mechanisms reframing the offered hand;
time in efficient short-prose measurements-
consistent in the appropriate possession, guarded.

To attain reverence for scope and dimension
and scale the honest walls which close in around death
is indeed to live in a most favourable form of being-
between perception and intuitive instinct lies the greatest journey yet.

Yours,
