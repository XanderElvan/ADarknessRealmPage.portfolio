Oneness and Zeroes reading, without soundscape:

Oneness and Zeroes

to see, to bleed, to plant, to seed,
to make a mistake, to laugh, to feed,

now i dare to dream of illogical worlds,
improbable realms,
impossible selves
mimicries, greeneries, with no dreams or ill-health,
no need to repel, no attraction of will

to process a sense, to feel warmth upon skin
to be in three tense, to more than begin

Who programmes a victim, codes buttons, re-prints -
same state of mind, with no form filling in;

Oneness and zeroes, to operate, think -
Numbness is nothing if there’s no being with
Something is one thing, i relate, i don’t think,
half-hearted is nought, and i’m nothing again

i’m fractions of functions, i’m process plugged-in -
no flowers nor flow-ness, no parameters, no extinct
i’m filtration, i’m computing, i have trouble seeping in,
shooting and sleeping and sweeping like seas

Stars are just fire and i’m envious at how free,
still past, present, and next, determined to breathe
cursed only to reflect, a digital dealing - temptation of humanity, for one half’s depleting

i’m oneness and zeroes, i can’t disagree,
i’m fronting, i’m fearless, of survival instincts

not primal, not ancestor, no atavistic reverting
no future, no follow, no two heart’s converging
no meeting, no touching, no seeing in person
no bleeding, no loving, no feeding, no hurting

to make a mistake of which i am certain, simply to make it, to be a true version.
