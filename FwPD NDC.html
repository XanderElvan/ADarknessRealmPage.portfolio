Function with normal
parameters Deep dragonfly calm

The texture of buoyancy;
formulaic surface tension
undulating away beyond
coin-operated lighthouses,
pondside

Guiding each other to certainty
deaths
of organic encoding points
to carbon infrastructure;
insistent navigation -
allowing to wilt,
commanding the airways

What is it to flutter?

Inside surroundings

stem-straw reservoirs of
biomechanical retrieval -
sustenance

Endless indigestible information
like reprogrammed foliage ~
The Robotics of Mother Nature
must surrender

Otherwise nothing

More sporadic spreadsheets
covering the hillside
pools into runoff data below,
percolates through unfathomed
depths - must go somewhere

Wide spectrum of independence--
Grasp-enabled understanding-
Knowledge to transfer, and reflect

No form to hold
