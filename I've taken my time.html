I've taken my time, I'm not where I want to be-


Through the passage of perception
elder star-coils wither in spiralling courses, run
inward-depletion wrung-out like damp space-cloth
erasing from the fabric of this realm, at least
a final circumstance of doing anything at all,
taking it in because - what else can you do -

Slip into dream-state consciousness
and blink out existence,
decide to disconnect the reality
you were holding together,
so for now - either you or I - is gone.

-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-

But this was never about that.
That bullet-holed blade of--
anyway..

Instead, grass-seeds pass us by,
free rides in fish-hook winds and
fibres from fleeces, their sleeves
strung across a waistline, knotted
at umbilical height, unneeded because
the sun comes like darkness feels

by the morning
all the insects and antennas out-scope
new worlds; one for every individual,
especially seeds of grass- who have
travelled farther than any ever could.
