Progressions in Amber

Spring's first breeze landing as seeds -
roots form orderly queues, breaking into life.

Light mineral-movements gesture carbon forms
purpose-served leaves waltz across.

Sunflowers breathe in the sunset;
a lifetime of gravity.

Launchingfrom pollen-cocooned limbs,
relative butterfly-agreements observe birdsongs

Soothing foliage limitations. Mottled sculptures
cradled by balconies, nested in negative space.

Mahogany silhouettes attach summits
as longevity-mountains yearn for starward journeys.
